MagicSusan by LabartCasa · Cognitive Furniture

Store it once. Ask for it later.

Magic Susan is a utility patent-pending cognitive furniture system from LabartCasa. The invention combines semantic retrieval and storage automation, so Susan can understand what you ask for and retrieve the right physical item.

Say “Hey Susan, give me my wallet” or “Hey Susan, give me something sharp” — and get it.

How it works

Place items inside. Susan learns each item using sensors and recognition. When you need something, just ask, and she gives you what you asked for.

Less searching, fewer “where did I put it?” moments, and faster daily routines.

Use cases

  • Grab your keys and wallet on the way out.
  • In the kitchen: “Hey Susan, give me something sharp.”
  • Find the right cable without digging through a drawer.
  • Quick access to glasses, chargers, small tools, or meds.
